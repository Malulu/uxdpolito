The project

We-Share is a project aimed to answer the issue of weather forecasting uncertanty. As a matter of fact, predicting weather behavior is getting crucial for many aspects of our society: from green energy production to the management of natural disasters. For this reason, We-Share offers more accurate weather data-sets based on a nationwide network of weather stations.

Our products

The We-Share platform is a crowd-sourced data network, gathering and integrating weather information, making it available on a web-based platform to support and enhance accurate local forecasts. We use existing infrastructures, and partner with public and private institutions, to place our proprietary sensors in strategic locations across the country.
